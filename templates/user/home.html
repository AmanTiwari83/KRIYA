ASHTANGA YOGA

The word Ashtanga is comprised of two sanskrit words, "Ashta" and "Anga". "Ashta" refers to the number eight , while "Anga" means limb or body parts. Since from 1990's Ashtanga has been considered one of the most popular forms of yoga in Westeren World.

HATHA YOGA

A yoga class described as "Hatha" will typically involve a set of physical posture. "Hatha" means force and is more traditionally defined as "Yoga of Force" or "The Meaning of Attaining a State of Yoga Through Force". It consider as anything you do with body.

IYENGAR YOGA

Iyengar yoga decends from the most classical Hatha yoga tradition. Iyengar Yoga focuses on three aspects : alignment ,sequencing and timing. In Iyengar Yoga when stability is achieved in a pose it's then possible to safely intensify the depth of posture.

KUNDALINI YOGA

Kundalini Yoga is a form of yoga that involves chanting , singing, breathing, exercise, and repetitive poses. As Kundalini yoga awakens this energy it's supposed to enhance your awareness and help you to move past year ago. It's also called "yoga of awareness"
